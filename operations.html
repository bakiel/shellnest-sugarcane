Our Model

Import & Export Trading

ShellNest operates as an import-export trading house for sugarcane-derived commodities. We purchase products from South African sugar mills and processors, then supply them to domestic buyers and international markets.

Based in Johannesburg, we are positioned at the commercial heart of South Africa's trading infrastructure. Our network extends across KwaZulu-Natal's sugar belt, where approximately 80% of the country's sugar is produced, as well as to international buyers across Africa, Asia, and the Middle East.

We handle all aspects of the trade -- from initial procurement and quality verification through to customs documentation, shipping arrangements, and final delivery to the buyer.

The Process

How a Trade Works

From enquiry to delivery, every trade follows a structured process to ensure quality, compliance, and timely fulfilment.

01

Enquiry & Specification

You tell us what you need -- product type, grade, volume, and delivery destination. We confirm availability and provide a competitive quotation based on current market conditions.

02

Procurement & Sourcing

We source the specified product from our network of South African sugar mills, refineries, and processors. Each supplier is vetted for quality, reliability, and compliance.

03

Quality Verification

Products are inspected and tested against agreed specifications before dispatch. Certificates of analysis, phytosanitary documents, and origin certificates are prepared as required.

04

Documentation & Compliance

We prepare all trade documentation including bills of lading, customs declarations, export permits, and letters of credit. Full compliance with South African and destination-country regulations.

05

Logistics & Delivery

Domestic deliveries via road transport. International shipments arranged through Durban port with full container load (FCL) or less-than-container (LCL) options available.
