Product Range

What We Trade

We supply the full range of sugarcane-derived commodities for domestic retail, food manufacturing, industrial applications, and international export.

Core Products

Primary Commodities

Three high-volume product categories that form the backbone of our trading operations.

Raw & Refined Sugar

White and brown sugar in bulk or bagged form. Sourced from South African mills, graded to international standards. Available in 25kg, 50kg bags, and bulk containers for export.

Molasses

Blackstrap and fancy-grade molasses for animal feed, fermentation, distilling, and food manufacturing. Supplied in bulk tanker loads or drummed for smaller orders.

Bio-Ethanol

Fuel-grade and industrial-grade ethanol distilled from molasses. Used in fuel blending, pharmaceutical production, industrial solvents, and sanitiser manufacturing.

How We Supply

From Mill to Market

We source directly from established sugar mills and processing facilities across KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa's primary sugarcane region. Every product we trade undergoes quality verification before dispatch.

Our logistics team coordinates transport from mill to port or direct to your facility. We handle all export documentation including phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, and customs declarations for international shipments.

Whether you are a food manufacturer needing regular sugar supply, a distiller seeking consistent molasses volumes, or an energy company sourcing bagasse -- we have the supply chain to deliver.

Complete Product Range

Every product in the sugarcane value chain -- available in volumes to suit your requirements.

White Sugar

Refined white sugar for retail, food processing, and beverage manufacturing. Available in 25kg and 50kg bags or bulk containers.

Brown Sugar

Natural brown sugar with retained molasses. Popular in baking, confectionery, and as a premium retail product for local and export markets.

Molasses

Blackstrap and fancy-grade molasses for animal feed, ethanol distilling, fermentation industries, and food manufacturing.

Bagasse

Fibrous residue from juice extraction. Traded as biomass fuel for co-generation of electricity and steam at industrial facilities.

Ethanol

Bio-ethanol for fuel blending, pharmaceutical applications, industrial solvents, and sanitiser production. Fuel-grade and industrial-grade available.

Jaggery

Traditional unrefined sugar with rich flavour and mineral content. A speciality product popular in local and regional markets for cooking and confectionery.
