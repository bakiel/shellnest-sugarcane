Who We Are

Sugarcane Product Trading

ShellNest is a Johannesburg-based import and export trading company specialising in sugarcane-derived commodities. We buy from South African mills and processors and supply to buyers across local and international markets.

Our product range includes raw and refined sugar, molasses, bio-ethanol, bagasse, and speciality products like jaggery. Whether you need bulk volumes for industrial use or smaller consignments for retail distribution, we handle procurement, documentation, and delivery.

What We Trade

Sugarcane Commodities

We trade across the full sugarcane product range -- from bulk raw sugar to speciality derivatives for industrial and retail markets.

Raw & Refined Sugar

Bulk white and brown sugar for domestic retail, food manufacturing, beverage production, and export markets. Sourced from South Africa's top mills.

Molasses & Ethanol

Molasses for animal feed and fermentation industries. Bio-ethanol for fuel blending, pharmaceutical applications, and industrial solvents.

Bagasse & Bio-Energy

Bagasse fibre for biomass energy generation. A key renewable energy feedstock for co-generation at sugar mills and independent power producers.
